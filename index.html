Investor Buy-In Option (Managed by Cosmic Development Corp.)

We currently have up to 50% of Terry’s shares available (up to 25% of the total project), plus the investor funds the outstanding work. Investor receives an equal proportion of: rental profit and sale proceeds/profit, based on ownership percentage.

Structure

  1. Completion funding: 300,000 THB goes directly into finishing the property.
  2. Equity purchase: Investor buys a % of the total project from Terry’s shares (up to 25%).
  3. Participation: Investor receives proportional rental + sale outcomes.

Example scenarios (editable)

Equity buy-in is shown at cost-basis valuation by default (2.8M THB / 100 = 28,000 THB per 1%). You can change the pricing logic in app.js.
